Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > CDL Exam Prep > Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Are the questions based on the real CDL exam?

Yes, our 435 questions cover the same topics and format as the official CDL written exam, including General Knowledge, Air Brakes, Combination Vehicle, and Hazardous Materials endorsements.

What score do I need to pass?

The passing score is 80% on each section, which mirrors the real CDL exam requirements in most states.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All questions and study content are available offline. An internet connection is only needed for purchasing or restoring subscriptions.

How many free questions do I get?

Free users get 10 General Knowledge questions per day. Subscribe to unlock all 435 questions across all 4 categories.
